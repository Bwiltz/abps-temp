American Board Of Plastic Surgery

Developing Effective Multiple-Choice Examination Items

Introduction

Thank you for generously volunteering your time and expertise to assist in the development of the ABPS examinations.

Your contribution will assure the rigorous assessment of candidates for inclusion in our profession.

The following workshop is intended to offer concrete guidelines to assist you in making efficient use of your time while developing effective examination items.

As you progress through this interactive workshop you will be provided opportunity to apply the stated guidelines to sample questions and item scenarios.

At the end of this online presentation you will be asked to complete a short summative evaluation exercise and the American Board Of Plastic Surgery will be notified of your successful completion of the workshop.
